HI! I'M ADDAGO.


My story
I am a Romanian designer who moved to The Netherlands in 2019 and has been living there ever since. My journey with design started from my passion for entrepreneurship, specifically when I had to visually present a poster and a prototype for a startup idea that I had in high school. In my free time I also take and edit photos, do photo manipulation, play video games and try to get educated about pretty much any subject I find interesting.


I see UX design as a creative journey in finding solutions to people's needs, both in the digital and physical world, listening to their pains and fulflling them by creating experiences that suit them. It's all about and around people and I believe it's the right place for me since it's what I've always been trying to do, even before I knew what the term UX meant.
