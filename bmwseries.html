BMW POSTER

This poster was inspired by the BMW brand itself.

As an automotive engineering enthusiast, I wanted to incorporate my passion for cars into the one for design. I used what I observed to be the most important traits that BMW uses as a base for their brand: minimalism and boldness. I made use of bold typography to balance the composition and put directly in focus the main product of the brand to showcase what I believe to be the holistic visualisation of the brand.
