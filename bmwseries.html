TShirt project
The IDEA behind the project

The full context has me as a starting designer and my best friend in the spotlights for how this idea came to life. Both being BMW brand fans and gearheads since we were children, I wanted to create something that had my signature and exploit this common passion of us.

The concept had to comply with the following:

• be WEARABLE (to be a friendship gift that can always be close)

• include AUTOMOTIVE and BMW

• be MINIMALIST

From establishing this baseline, it was just a matter of creativity spark in a split second that made the connection between the distinctive features of the car for the front and back and the sides of a tshirt.

It just HAPPENED...

Urban Vectorized Poster
poster inspired by Bram Vanhaeren


The IDEA behind the project

The vectorizing tool found in Adobe Illustrator is definitely a powerful one which I wanted to explore in detail and make full use of its complexity. I based this idea on converting a car into displayable art that would communicate the boldness of the brand and of the model, a sport series M model created for power.

The concept had to comply with the following:

• make use of the VECTORIZING TOOL to the max

• target the MILLENIALS style

• be BOLD and URBAN
